Transformers Architecture Overview

📅 Updated Feb 24, 2026 ⏱ 12 min read 👁 4.2k views

The Transformer architecture, introduced in the seminal paper "Attention Is All You Need" by Vaswani et al. (2017), revolutionized the field of natural language processing and has since become the backbone of modern large language models. Unlike recurrent neural networks, Transformers process all positions in a sequence simultaneously through self-attention mechanisms.
